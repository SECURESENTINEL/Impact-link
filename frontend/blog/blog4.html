Student Volunteering

Volunteering helps students grow both personally and professionally. It improves communication skills, builds confidence, and develops a sense of responsibility.

From a career perspective, volunteering adds value to a resume by showing initiative, teamwork, and social awareness. Many employers and universities appreciate candidates who actively contribute to society.

By joining volunteering activities through an NGO platform, students can balance academics with social responsibility and gain real-world exposure.
