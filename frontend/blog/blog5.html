Volunteering Basics

With the rise of technology, volunteering is no longer limited to physical locations. Online volunteering allows individuals to contribute from anywhere using digital skills.

Activities such as content writing, social media management, teaching online, and graphic designing can be done remotely. This makes volunteering accessible to students who have limited time or mobility.

NGO volunteering platforms help match online volunteers with suitable NGOs, making social work more flexible and inclusive
